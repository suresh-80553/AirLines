About Safety

Airlines have well-defined emergency procedures in place to handle various situations, including equipment malfunctions, severe weather, and medical emergencies. Crew members receive training on emergency evacuation, fire suppression, first aid, and other relevant procedures.

Airlines prioritize a strong safety culture, emphasizing the importance of safety at all levels of the organization. This includes promoting reporting of safety concerns, encouraging open communication, and fostering a proactive approach to safety.
